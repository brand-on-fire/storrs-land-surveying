About Storrs Land Surveying

Storrs Land Surveying is a California-licensed land surveying firm (License No. 6914) serving residential property owners and land developers with accurate, reliable surveying services. Whether you’re planning a custom home, subdividing a parcel, converting a property to condominiums, or developing a site, we offer the experience and precision to move your project forward with confidence.

Our services include but are not limited to; boundary surveys, topographic surveys, construction staking, subdivision mapping, condominium conversions, lot line adjustments, legal description preparation, and ALTA/NSPS Land Title Surveys. We understand the details that matter in residential and commercial land development and deliver clear, timely results tailored to your needs.

We also provide expert witness services for boundary disputes, encroachments, and land use litigation. Our team is skilled in presenting technical findings clearly and professionally in legal settings.

At Storrs Land Surveying, we combine deep local knowledge, modern tools, and a client-first mindset to help you make informed, confident decisions—every step of the way.
